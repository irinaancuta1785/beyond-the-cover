Discussion Guide

Every great book deserves a great conversation. Share what moved you, question what puzzled you, and explore the layers you might have missed. Listen deeply to different perspectives and discover how the same story can touch each reader differently. Whether you loved it or struggled with it, your voice adds richness to our literary journey. Bring your thoughts, your favorite passages, and your curiosity—let's explore these worlds together.
